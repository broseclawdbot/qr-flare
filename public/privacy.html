Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

QR Flare is built by Volume Entertainment. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

QR Flare does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All QR codes are generated locally on your device. We do not require account creation or sign-in.

Photo Library Access

QR Flare requests permission to access your photo library solely to save generated QR codes. Photos are saved directly to your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Third-Party Services

QR Flare does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Data Storage

All data you enter into QR Flare (URLs, text, Wi-Fi credentials, etc.) is processed entirely on your device and is not stored, logged, or transmitted anywhere.

Children's Privacy

QR Flare does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
